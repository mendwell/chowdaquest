How rankings work

Every score starts with a real review

A restaurant joins the standings after receiving a complete review for the selected food category.

Its overall rating averages flavor, featured ingredient quantity, freshness, value, portion, and Cross the Bridge Score.

We also show the Cross the Bridge Score on its own—the distinctly Rhode Island question: would you cross the bridge for it?

Restaurants waiting for their first review remain listed below the standings, making it easy to see where fresh reports are needed.
