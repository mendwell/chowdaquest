How it works

Honest reviews only

Restaurants enter the rankings after receiving at least one complete review in the selected category.

The main ranking averages flavor, featured ingredient quantity, freshness, value, portion, and Cross the Bridge Score.

Cross the Bridge Score is also shown separately as the Rhode Island gut-check: would you cross the bridge for it?

Unreviewed restaurants stay visible below the standings so inspectors know where reports are still needed.
